Raceform Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2026

Raceform is a brand and digital service operated by Paivio Media. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

1. What data we collect

If you choose to connect third-party platforms such as Garmin, we may collect and process data you explicitly authorize us to access. This may include activity data, workout data, training metrics, wellness data, and related performance information.

2. How we use your data

We use your data to provide Raceform services, including training analytics, dashboards, performance insights, progress tracking, coaching-related features, and product improvements.

3. Legal basis and consent

We only access third-party fitness or activity data when you have given clear consent by connecting your account. You may revoke access at any time by disconnecting the integration.

4. Data sharing

We do not sell your personal activity or health-related data. We only share data with service providers where necessary to operate the Raceform service, comply with legal obligations, or protect our rights.

5. Data retention

We retain personal data only for as long as needed to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

6. Data security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal data against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

7. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to the processing of your personal data. You may also request information about the data we hold about you.

8. Third-party integrations

If you connect Garmin or other third-party platforms, your use of those services is also subject to their own terms and privacy policies.
